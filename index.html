Arman Afrasiyabi
Ph.D., Researcher |

Interested in meta-learning representations, I am a postdoc researcher at KrishnaswamyLab of Yale University. In 2022, I received my Ph.D. from Université Laval & Mila, advised by Jean-François Lalonde & Christian Gagné. Three contributions of my Ph.D. have been published at ECCV-2020, ICCV-2021, and CVPR-2022. Before, I was with ImageLab and got my M.S. from METU. I was born and raised in Band, a beautiful village on Pale Blue Dot.
